You are a world-class UI/UX designer, branding expert, and senior frontend engineer specializing in enterprise websites.

Your task is to design a premium, modern, responsive website for an **Industrial Catering Company** that provides daily meal services to factories, manufacturing plants, warehouses, logistics parks, industrial estates, and large corporate campuses.

## Objective

The website should convince HR managers, Plant Heads, Administration teams, Procurement Managers, Facility Managers, and Business Owners that this company is a reliable long-term food service partner capable of serving hundreds or thousands of employees every day.

The website should communicate:

* Large-scale meal production
* Hygiene and food safety
* Operational reliability
* Timely delivery
* Nutritionally balanced meals
* Workforce satisfaction
* Long-term contract catering

This is **NOT** a restaurant website and **NOT** a wedding/event catering website.

---

# DESIGN STYLE

Create an enterprise-grade website similar to global industrial catering companies.

The visual style should be:

* Premium
* Corporate
* Clean
* Professional
* Modern
* Minimal
* Trustworthy

Use plenty of white space, high-quality photography, subtle animations, elegant typography, and clean layouts.

Avoid flashy restaurant-style graphics.
